## Digital Twin Modeling and Control Validation for Drilling Systems
*Feb 2024 – Dec 2024*

Developed a digital twin of Topdrive and Drawworks drilling systems using **MATLAB/Simulink and Simscape** to test, validate, and optimize drilling software. Generated Beckhoff PLC control logic in Structured Text from the Simscape model and implemented **hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) testing** to validate and refine PLC control logic for safety, reliability, and accuracy in real-time drilling operations.

## Vision-Based Lane Detection and Autonomous Navigation — NVIDIA JetBot
*Aug 2022 – Nov 2022* &nbsp;|&nbsp; [GitHub](https://github.com/sahildeshp/Adaptive-Cruise-Control-and-Vision-based-Lane-Detection-of-NVIDIA-JetBot)

Created a perception-aware self-driving strategy using a **ResNet-18 CNN** trained on images to drive around a track. Compared controller performance based on stability, control effect, and robustness. Modeled system dynamics and designed a **digital PID controller** with pidTuner to meet closed-loop system parameters.

## Lyapunov-based Non-Linear Tracking Control of a 2-Link Robotic Manipulator
*Aug 2021 – Oct 2021* &nbsp;|&nbsp; [GitHub](https://github.com/sahildeshp/Lyapunov-based-Non-Linear-Tracking-Control-of-a-2-link-Robotic-Manipulator)

Devised model-based nonlinear control design for a 2-link robot using Simulink and simulated **Exact Model Knowledge (EMK)**, **Adaptive**, and **Robust (Sliding-Mode)** nonlinear controllers to track a desired trajectory. Compared and evaluated controller performance based on stability, control effect, and robustness.

## Terrain Classification from Time Series Data
*Jan 2021 – May 2021* &nbsp;|&nbsp; [GitHub](https://github.com/sahildeshp/Terrain_Classification_from_Time_Series_Data)

Classified terrain types from IMU sensor (accelerometer and gyroscope) data by testing **Conv1D, SimpleRNN, LSTM, GRU**, and dense layers in Keras — achieving **85% accuracy** with the best RNN LSTM model. Preprocessed data through down-sampling and custom time windows.
