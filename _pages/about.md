I am a Controls Software Engineer at **Siemens Energy**, specializing in model-based controls design for HVDC (High Voltage Direct Current) systems that power sustainable energy grids.

I completed my graduate studies at **North Carolina State University**, where I worked on advanced control systems, autonomous robotics, and neural networks. Prior to that, I earned my undergraduate degree from **Savitribai Phule Pune University**.

My technical work spans MATLAB/Simulink, Stateflow, Beckhoff TwinCAT, IEC 61850, Python, and model-based systems engineering (MBSE). I am passionate about building reliable, efficient control systems that contribute to the global clean energy transition.
